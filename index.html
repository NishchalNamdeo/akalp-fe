What is Digital Marketing?

▼

Digital marketing refers to the use of online channels and technologies to promote products or services and engage with customers. It encompasses a variety of strategies and tactics.

How it can be done?

▼

Digital marketing can be effectively done by developing a strategic plan, building an optimized online presence, utilizing social media, creating valuable content, engaging in email marketing, running targeted PPC ads, leveraging analytics, and staying updated on industry trends.

How much it costs?

▼

Digital marketing The cost of digital marketing varies depending on the strategy and scale of the campaign, ranging from a few hundred dollars for basic services to thousands for comprehensive campaigns.
